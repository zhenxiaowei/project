Global Journey of Antenna Modernization @ Latin America

Latin America Antenna Night 2016

Antenna night has been staged by Huawei, on the evening of Tuesday, April 5th, in the Sulamerica Convention Center, Rio de Janeiro, it was around the "Antenna Modernization, Bridging the Way to Giga Mobile as the main theme" .which attracted more than 90 person to attend, including operators, industry standards organizations, and ICT media, who together discussed 4.5G-oriented antenna chances and challenges.

During the evening event, 3 regional operators shared the successful experiences on antenna deployment, in addition, Huawei launched the 4.5G-oriented antenna solution for LATAM, which had been widely covered by press in various countries, such as Brazil, Argentina, Peru, Columbia and others.
