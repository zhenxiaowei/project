Global Journey of Antenna Modernization @ Middle East and North Africa

Antenna & AAU @ 5G World MENA 2016

On May 9th, LTE & 5G MENA Summit was staged by Informa. As the beginning of the summit, the Antenna and AAU focus day was jointly held by Informa and Huawei in the Conrad hotel. The event attracted more than 70 persons to attend, including operator representatives and industry research and consulting organizations (Arab).

During the event, Huawei launched the 4.5G-oriented antenna solution for MENA. The operators showed great interest in the antenna and AAU innovative solution exhibition and discussed with Huawei experts about the antenna network deployment.
